Grizzly Bear

Unlike many mammals, bears can see in color.

  • Scientific Name: Ursidae
  • Average Height: 2 feet 4 inches - 5 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 20-25 years
  • Habitat: Mountainous Woodlands

Grizzly bears have long, round claws and a large mass of muscles on their backs that help them dig. These Characteristics, along with small rounded ears and white-tipped fur, distinguish grizzly bears from black bears.
